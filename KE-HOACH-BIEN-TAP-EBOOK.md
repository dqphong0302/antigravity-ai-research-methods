# Ke Hoach Bien Tap Ebook Antigravity

## 1. Muc tieu bien tap

Muc tieu cua lan viet lai nay khong chi la "them chu" ma la nang cuon ebook tu muc so tay cong cu len muc mot tac pham huong dan nghien cuu co luc, co chieu sau, va co thong diep ro rang.

Ba dich den chinh:

- Viet chi tiet hon: moi chuong du do day de nguoi doc co the thuc su di tu "hieu" sang "lam duoc".
- Viet chin chu hon: thong nhat giong van, logic, muc tieu hoc tap, va cau truc bien tap giua cac chuong.
- Viet co y nghia hon: nhan manh AI khong thay the nha nghien cuu, ma giup con nguoi gianh lai thoi gian cho tu duy, dao duc, va dong gop hoc thuat.

## 2. Chan doan nhanh sau khi ra soat

### Diem manh hien tai

- Khung sach da rat ro: di theo vong doi nghien cuu tu y tuong den cong bo.
- Giong van than thien, de tiep can, co tinh huong thuc hanh.
- Chuong 2, 3, 4 va mot phan chuong 7 cho thay huong viet tot: co giai thich, co prompt, co quy trinh, co bai tap.
- Sach da co dinh huong dao duc ro rang, khong sa vao cach dung AI thay nguoi.

### Van de lon can xu ly

1. Do dai cac chuong rat lech nhau.
   Co chuong hon 2.100 tu, nhung co chuong chi khoang 500-700 tu. Dieu nay lam ebook bi "hut hoi" o nua sau.

2. Nhieu chuong dang o dang checklist/prompt list.
   Noi dung huu ich nhung chua du day de tao cam giac mot cuon sach co lap luan va chieu sau.

3. Thieu mot thong diep xuyen suot.
   Sach dang manh ve "dung cong cu nao" hon la "vi sao cach lam nay giup nghien cuu tot hon, co trach nhiem hon".

4. Chua co nhan vat hoac case xuyen chuong.
   Vi du co, nhung dang nam rac. Neu co 1-2 de tai mau di xuyen suot sach, nguoi doc se bam theo tot hon.

5. Boi canh Viet Nam moi xuat hien tung diem.
   Can tang tinh dia phuong: van hoa hoc thuat, thuc te hoc vien, gioi han nguon luc, yeu cau cua GVHD, hoi dong, va tap chi.

6. Mot so chuong cuoi qua ngan so voi tam quan trong cua chu de.
   Dac biet: chuong 8, 9, 12, 13, 14, 15.

7. Muc luc chua dong bo voi file that.
   `README.md` dang tro toi phu luc FAQ va Case Studies khong ton tai theo ten dang viet.

## 3. Dinh vi lai cuon sach

De ebook "co y nghia", can chot mot luan de xuyen suot:

> AI khong lam nghien cuu thay ban. AI giai phong ban khoi nhung lao dong lap di lap lai, de ban dau tu nhieu hon vao cau hoi khoa hoc, phan doan hoc thuat, va trach nhiem voi tri thuc.

Tu luan de nay, moi phan cua sach can tra loi 3 cau hoi:

- Buoc nay quan trong gi trong hanh trinh nghien cuu?
- AI ho tro duoc gi ma van giu vai tro trung tam cua nha nghien cuu?
- Sai lam nao de xay ra neu lam qua nhanh, qua tin AI, hoac bo qua kiem chung?

## 4. Khung viet moi cho moi chuong

De sach dong deu, moi chuong nen duoc viet theo khung 9 phan duoi day:

1. Mo chuong bang mot van de that.
   Mot tinh huong, mot kho khan, hoac mot cau hoi ma nguoi lam nghien cuu thuong gap.

2. Giai thich y nghia hoc thuat cua buoc nay.
   Khong chi noi "lam nhu the nao", ma phai noi "vi sao buoc nay quyet dinh chat luong cong trinh".

3. Trinh bay sai lam pho bien.
   Neu nguoi doc thay duoc nhung bay tac nghien cuu, ebook se thuc te hon.

4. Dua vao workflow voi Antigravity.
   Day la phan cong cu, prompt, quy trinh thuc hanh.

5. Chay song song 2 vi du.
   Mot vi du STEM va mot vi du khoa hoc xa hoi/giao duc.

6. Chen "diem dung de suy nghi".
   Goi y nguoi doc tu hoi: du lieu nay co dang tin khong, gia thuyet nay co qua vo tinh khong, prompt nay co gay lech khong.

7. Them hop "dao duc va gioi han".
   Moi chuong deu nen co mot doan ngan ve rui ro, hallucination, bao mat, hoac lam dung.

8. Ket chuong bang deliverable ro rang.
   Nguoi doc hoc xong chuong phai tao duoc mot san pham: research question, ma tran tai lieu, framework, instrument draft, analysis memo, defense outline.

9. Co phan tong ket 5 dong.
   Chot lai thong diep, va noi ro chuong tiep theo se noi gi.

## 6. Huong mo rong cu the theo chuong

### Chuong 8: Nghien cuu hon hop

Can viet lai gan nhu tu dau. Chuong nay dang dung y nhung qua ngan.

Can bo sung:

- Vi sao mixed methods khong chi la "lam ca hai".
- Cac logic tich hop: merge, connect, build, embed.
- Tinh huong nao nen dung convergent, explanatory, exploratory.
- Rui ro lon nhat: hai nhanh khong thuc su noi vao nhau.
- Joint display mau day du.
- Mot case Viet Nam di tron quy trinh.

### Chuong 9: Thu thap du lieu

Can mo rong theo huong "quan tri du lieu nghien cuu", khong chi la scraping va OCR.

Can bo sung:

- Consent, anonymization, data dictionary, metadata.
- Phan biet du lieu so cap, thu cap, tai lieu so hoa, du lieu nhay cam.
- Workflow luu tru co the tai lap.
- Muc "khi nao khong nen tu dong hoa".

### Chuong 12: Visualization

Can doi tu "cach ve" thanh "cach ke chuyen bang du lieu".

Can bo sung:

- Mot thong diep, mot figure.
- Chon bieu do theo cau hoi nghien cuu.
- Loi trinh bay pho bien trong bai bao va bao ve.
- Figure caption, visual ethics, mau sac, accessibility.
- Vi du chuyen mot ket qua khong ro thanh hinh de hieu.

### Chuong 13: Viet bai bao khoa hoc

Can day them phan tu duy viet va lap luan hoc thuat.

Can bo sung:

- Logic dong van o Introduction va Discussion.
- Su khac nhau giua report ket qua va lap luan dong gop.
- Cach viet literature gap de khong "ke ten bai bao".
- Cach xu ly reviewer comments.
- Mau bang doi chieu journal target.

### Chuong 14: Viet luan van/luan an

Chuong nay dang qua mong so voi nhu cau thuc.

Can bo sung:

- Quan he giua bai bao va luan van.
- Cach lap lich viet 6-12 thang.
- Quy trinh lam viec voi GVHD, ghi nhan feedback, va versioning.
- Khung chuong chi tiet cho thac si va tien si.
- Tam ly va ky luat cua du an dai han.
- Chuan bi bao ve: script, slide, Q&A, tinh huong kho.

### Chuong 15: Truyen thong khoa hoc

Can them "tai sao phai truyen thong" va "truyen thong co trach nhiem".

Can bo sung:

- Xac dinh audience: hoc thuat, quan ly, cong chung.
- Mot nghien cuu, nhieu dinh dang, nhieu muc dich.
- Cach tranh giat tit, don gian hoa qua muc.
- Vi du chuyen bai bao thanh brief, podcast, slide, post.

## 7. Cac lop noi dung can them de ebook co chieu sau

### Lop 1: Hanh trinh nguoi doc

Nen them 2 nhan vat mau xuyen suot:

- Mot nghien cuu sinh giao duc/STEM
- Mot hoc vien cao hoc hoac nha nghien cuu xa hoi hoc

Moi chuong deu quay lai 2 de tai nay de cho thay cach ung dung that.

### Lop 2: Tu duy phuong phap

Sau moi workflow cong cu, can chen 1 doan ngan:

- Dieu gi la phan doan cua con nguoi?
- Dieu gi khong duoc khoan trang cho AI?
- Dau la tieu chuan chat luong cua output?

### Lop 3: Boi canh Viet Nam

Can tang mat do nhac den:

- Dieu kien lam nghien cuu trong boi canh thieu nguon luc
- Ky vong cua GVHD va hoi dong
- Van de ngon ngu Anh - Viet trong viet hoc thuat
- Tinh nhay cam cua du lieu giao duc, y te, va khu vuc cong

## 9. Tieu chuan chat luong moi khi viet lai

Moi chuong sau khi sua xong can qua 8 cau hoi kiem:

1. Chuong nay co mo ra mot van de that khong?
2. Nguoi doc co hieu vi sao buoc nay quan trong khong?
3. Chuong co vuot qua muc liet ke cong cu khong?
4. Co it nhat 2 vi du cu the khong?
5. Co noi ro viec gi con nguoi phai tu quyet khong?
6. Co nhac toi gioi han va kiem chung AI khong?
7. Hoc xong chuong, nguoi doc tao duoc deliverable nao?
8. Chuong co de lai mot y nghia lon hon viec "lam nhanh hon" khong?

## 10. Cau chot bien tap

Neu giu khung hien tai va chi them prompt, ebook se huu ich nhung kho tro thanh mot cuon sach nho lau.

Neu viet lai theo huong tren, ebook se co 3 gia tri ro hon:

- Gia tri thuc hanh: nguoi doc dung duoc ngay.
- Gia tri hoc thuat: nguoi doc hieu sau hon ve logic nghien cuu.
- Gia tri nhan van: nguoi doc thay AI phuc vu cho tu duy trung thuc, can trong, va co trach nhiem.

## 11. Buoc tiep theo de xuat

Thu tu lam viec toi uu:

1. Chot thong diep xuyen suot cho toan sach.
2. Viet lai Chuong 8 va Chuong 14 truoc.
3. Chuan hoa mau chuong cho cac chuong con lai.
4. Sau do moi day them prompt cookbook va phu luc.

Neu tiep tuc o vong sau, nen lam theo cach "viet lai tung chuong", khong nen chinh tat ca cung luc.
